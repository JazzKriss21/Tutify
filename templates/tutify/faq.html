    FAQs

    • How can I (student) contact tutors?

      You can search for tutors and if you find one you can send a request through our website. Or you can directly contact with the contact info given by tutors.

    • How are ratings given to tutors?

      Ratings are given by students through our website. No one has permision to modify it.

    • Can I teach more than one subject/skill?

      Yes, You can add new subject skill after login.

    • How should I pay tutors?

      Paying tutors is completely dependent on tutors. You should contact your tutor for this matter.

    • What if tutor is lied about his subject/skill knowledge?

      You can report them and strict action will be taken.

    • Are online tutors available?

      Yes, Online tutors are available but mode of comunication will be decided by respective tutors.
